Our Story

About the Masjid

Masjid e Jadeed has been serving as a spiritual home and community center since 1999. Rooted in faith and service, we welcome Muslims and visitors of all backgrounds to experience the warmth of our community.

Our mission is to provide a serene space for worship, foster Islamic education, and build bridges of understanding with the wider community. We are also providing monthly groceries to the needy in our area since 2020. We also run a Madrasa providing Islamic Education in alliance with DEENIYAT.
